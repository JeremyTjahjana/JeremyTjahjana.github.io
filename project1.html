Project 1 - Right Over

RightOver is an app that helps you get creative in the kitchen by giving fun and healthy recipe ideas based on the ingredients you already have. Instead of letting leftovers go to waste, the app suggests meals that are easy to make and good for you. Built with Swift, RightOver makes cooking simple and enjoyable, helping you turn what’s left in the fridge into something delicious and satisfying—no stress, just great food!
